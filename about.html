About Us

Live Tech and Healthcare is a digital online (Web and Mobile App) platform for quality healthcare delivery and maternal and child health services. With this platform, patients can book for medical consultations any time with qualified medical doctors and other healthcare specialists, have their laboratory results reviewed and their prescribed medications delivered to them at the comfort of their homes.


It will also improve the efficient and quality maternal and child health services by sending periodic reminders to pregnant women about how to spot danger signs in pregnancy, take their routine medications, when to visit ANC and their expected date of delivery. For girls and women in the developing countries like Ghana, inequality is amplified. Poverty is sexist, Live Tech and Healthcare is adding its voice to the people calling for change. We will encourage the public to take control over their lives and improve their health and wellness by using available local resources.


We have realized that maternal and child health services have not been efficiently improved and most people do not visit hospitals when sick due to long waiting periods at the hospitals, high hospitals bills and bad attitude of some healthcare personals. This calls for an intervention that will allow patients and pregnant women have all their medical needs attended to at the comfort of their homes. With an innovative technology, pregnant women and patients will received periodic notifications via email, SMS, automated phone calls about the status of their health.


With our innovative technology, specialists will earn money, patients will save time, energy and money and pregnant women will have all the necessary and accurate information regarding their health, all information will be accurately tracked and recorded. Live Tech and healthcare deals with advance healthcare aims at offering a unique and seamless home, corporate and online health care services for pregnant women and newborns, and the general public. We believe that by connecting patients to competent, dedicated, well-trained and qualified medical professionals, who are responsive to the needs of our clients, their families and the community we serve, we will become the corporate, home, and online healthcare provider of choice in Ghana and beyond. Our clients will be provided with an individualized care plan, implemented and evaluated by experienced experts through weekly visits, phone calls and online consultations. At Live Tech and Healthcare, we believed that all women, pregnant women and newborns must live in victory always.
