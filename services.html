This is our services page

The support is flexible and our team provides all the help needed for you to remain in your home. This include personal care, warming up meals for clients, tiding up immediate surroundings of client. Chaperoning clients/patients to hospital appointment, companionship, administration of medication, activities in and out of the home. We offer a 24-hour individualized healthcare and nursing services, 7 days a week. All our nurses are certified, trained and supervised by a registered nurse in charge. We have registered nurses and healthcare assistants.
