Maternal and Child Health Services

To provide efficient and effective services to improve healthcare deliveries for pregnant women, newborns and children. Many women and newborns die during pregnancy, delivery and after delivery from conditions that could be easily prevented. The conditions include severe bleeding, infections, high blood pressure during pregnancy, complications from delivery, unsafe abortion among other causes.


Most pregnant women and newborns do not have access quality care because of poverty, long distance, lack of information, inadequate of quality services. Live Tech and Healthcare will provide quality and affordable health care, bridging the long the long distance by offering online consultations and frequent home visits to pregnant women. With an innovative technology, women will received reminder through SMS, emails, and automated phone calls about their medications, ANC visit, and information on how to have a healthy pregnancy.


They will also receive periodic advices and education on birth preparedness plan, delivery, breastfeeding, immunizations, nutrition, exercise, rest and sleep, how to spot danger signs in pregnancy, signs of labour, expected date of delivery. This will enable them to have their births attended to by a skilled health personnel to prevent complications and infections.


The progression of pregnancy right from conception to delivery will be accurately tracked and recorded. They will have access to accurate information about comprehensive family planning, prevention of mother to child transmission of HIV/AIDS and STIs. They will be empowered to make informed decisions concerning their reproductive rights.


At live Tech and Healthcare, believe that pregnancy and child birth is a basic human right and women and children need to have access to efficient, quality and affordable healthcare before, during and after pregnancy.
